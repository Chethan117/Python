<li>Merchants are grouped into three categories: recoverable (money that can be recovered), non-recoverable (money that is lost), and all transactions (a combination of both).</li><li>The transaction counts and amounts are calculated for daily, weekly, monthly, and rolling 30-day periods.</li><li>Merchants are ranked based on the highest transaction amounts and the total number of records, with ties broken alphabetically by merchant name.</li><li>Old data in the department table is deleted to ensure only fresh data is available.</li><li>The top 100 merchants for each category and time frame are combined and inserted into the department table for reporting and visualization.
