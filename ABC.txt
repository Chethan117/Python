The process involves creating a temporary table to calculate the total fraud amounts for each month, up to the current date. First, the start date of each month is identified, and key details like transaction type, fraud type, and amounts are selected. A running total of the amounts is calculated for each month, excluding future dates to ensure accuracy. The data is filtered to include only the last 13 months. After calculating the month-to-date totals, old results in the department table are deleted to avoid duplication. The newly calculated data is then pushed into the department table in Snowflake, where it can be visualized in Tableau for easier tracking and reporting of trend
